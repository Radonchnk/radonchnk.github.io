Monobank is a widely used online banking app in Ukraine, with around ten million users,
approximately a quarter of the country’s population . Its creators have built a system
somewhat similar to Monzo, a well-known online bank in the UK.
Balancing security and usability is extremely important. As is well known, users, like
water, tend to follow the path of least resistance. It is therefore the developer’s responsibility
to design systems in such a way that user convenience or laziness does not compromise
security.
In this report, I will examine the usability and security risks of the money transfer and
payee addition features, referencing.

This is a coursework that was submitted as part of a Human Behaviour in Cyber Systems module from Year 2 as a part of the Cyber Security Degree at University of Warwick
