The purpose of this project is to develop a network that meets the company’s requirements efficiently while maintaining scalability for future expansion at a reasonable cost.
We are required to design a network that provides reliable communication across multiple floors, with each floor hosting different departments. The network is going to have 150+ devices, with potential for future expansion. The network must be secure, with proper IP addressing and routing protocols configured to ensure seamless communication between devices.


This is a coursework that was submitted as part of a Networks Communications and Cyber Defence module from Year 1 as a part of the Cyber Security Degree at University of Warwick
