> **Scenario:** A secure and well-structured network is essential for **Tech Zolutions Inc.**, a software development company with 100 employees across three departments: Sales & Marketing (30 hosts), Development (50 hosts), and IT (20 hosts). The company also maintains a server room (hosting Web, DHCP, and Email servers) and a conference room with a video conferencing system.
{: .prompt-info }

This project focuses on designing and implementing a secure network that ensures efficient communication, resource allocation, and security. The implementation is divided into three key areas:

*   **Network Design and Configuration** – Developing a network topology, performing subnetting, and configuring routers, switches, and DHCP.
*   **Traffic Analysis** – Capturing and analysing network traffic to understand internal and inter-department communication.
*   **Security Implementation** – Implementing access control measures, configuring a Zone-Policy Firewall (ZPF), Access Control List (ACL), and segmenting the network into zones of trust.

The final design ensures secure, scalable, and reliable connectivity, meeting the company’s operational needs while protecting critical resources.


This is a coursework that was submitted as part of a Networks Communications and Cyber Defence module from Year 1 as a part of the Cyber Security Degree at University of Warwick
