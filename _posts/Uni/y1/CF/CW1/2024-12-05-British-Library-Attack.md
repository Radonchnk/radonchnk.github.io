This report analyses the rising frequency and impact of cyber attacks
on UK higher education. Astonishingly, 97% of universities faced cyber
attacks in 2023–2024, with weekly attacks reported by 43% of univer-
sities. Notable cases include DDoS attacks and ransomware targeting
Cambridge University, highlighting the destructive capabilities of unso-
phisticated actors. The report also analyses the October 2023 British
Library cyber attack by Rhysida, revealing weaknesses in legacy sys-
tems, network complexity, and security practices. Recommendations
include modernizing infrastructure, enhancing network segmentation,
and improving staff cyber security training to build resilience against
future threats.


This is a coursework that was submitted as part of a Cyber Fundamentals Module from Year 1 as a part of the Cyber Security Degree at University of Warwick
